## Updated Color Palette

The color scheme has been updated to match the official SkyRescue Presentation Deck:

### Primary Colors
- **Sky Blue:** `#058DC7` (was `#00AEEF`)
  - Primary brand color
  - Headers, buttons, links
  - Trust and calmness

- **Green:** `#50B432` (was `#39FF14` neon green)
  - Accent color
  - Call-to-action elements
  - Medical vitality, life-saving action

- **Dark Gray-Blue:** `#158158` (was `#333333` charcoal)
  - Text and professional contrast
  - More sophisticated than pure black

### Supporting Colors
- **White:** `#FFFFFF` - Clean and clear (unchanged)
- **Cyan:** `#24CBE5` - Interactive highlights and hover states
- **Light Green:** `#64E572` - Success indicators and positive feedback
- **Orange:** `#ED561B` - Alerts, warnings, and urgent actions
